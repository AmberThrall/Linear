\mbox{\hyperlink{namespace_linear}{Linear}} is a C++14 linear algebra all-\/header library.\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}{}\doxysection{Features}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Statically or dynamically sized matrices
\item Multiple matrix decomposition methods, including\+: LUP, QR, Cholesky, Eigen, SVD and Schur
\item Eigenvalue and eigenvector methods
\item Equation solving of the form Ax=b
\item Vectors with a distinction between row and column vectors
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md2}{}\doxysection{Installation}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md2}
